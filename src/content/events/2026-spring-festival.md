Join us for the 23rd Annual Japanese Spring Festival at the North Alabama Japanese Garden. The garden's azaleas should be in bloom.

**What to expect:**
- Traditional Japanese tea ceremony at the Tea House
- Taiko drumming by Daikin's Taiko Force
- Martial arts demonstrations (karate, kendo)
- Calligraphy and origami for all ages
- Japanese dance
- Japanese food vendors

**Admission:** $10 per vehicle (replaces normal park entrance fee).

**Parking:** Near the Monte Sano Lodge. Arrive early for best parking.
